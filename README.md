# ☕ Coffee Shop Product Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project is a data analysis and visualization dashboard built to explore coffee shop sales performance. It provides insights into product revenue, sales volume, customer purchasing behavior, and peak sales hours. The goal is to help identify best-selling products, understand customer preferences, and support data-driven business decisions.

## 🧰 Tools Used

* Tableau (Data Visualization)
* Kaggle (Datasets)
* Data Analysis Techniques (Aggregation, Trend Analysis)

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Revenue per Product

* Identifies top revenue-generating coffee products.
* Highlights which items contribute most to overall sales.

### 2. Number of Coffee Sold

* Shows product popularity based on total quantity sold.
* Helps compare demand across different coffee types.

### 3. Sales Trend by Hour

* Analyzes customer purchasing behavior throughout the day.
* Identifies peak hours for top-selling products.

### 4. Customer Payment Type

* Displays distribution of payment methods (cash vs card).
* Helps understand customer payment preferences.

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Latte and Americano with Milk generate the highest revenue and sales volume.
* Morning hours (around 8 AM – 11 AM) show peak product demand.
* Sales gradually decrease in the evening, with occasional small spikes.
* Card payments dominate over cash transactions.

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze coffee shop sales data and transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that can help improve product strategy, staffing decisions, and sales performance.
